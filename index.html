Tamarind Hall Cluster

Introduction

Located right next to a forest, Tamarind hall cluster is one of the newest hall clusters in Nanyang Technological University (NTU). It boasts of state-of-the-art gym, a canteen with a wide variety and many amenities to make living here comfortable. With both the campus red line and SBS 199 at the nearby bus stop, it is very accessible too!

Rooms in Tamarind Hall

Both our single and double rooms have plenty of spaces and provides the basic necessities such as a bed, study table and a ceiling fan.

Canteen

With Singapore being a food paradise, we are proud to say that our canteen also covers many different cuisines from around the world!

Lounge

We also have lounge rooms to cater to our residents' social needs, which comes with air-conditioning, TV and a sofa!

Lounge

Lastly, we have a multipurpose hall to hold all our major events, such as the annual AGM, CNY, Exam welfare pack giveaways!
